Decoration & Organizing Event

In India, wedding planners are often considered an ‘extra expenditure’ at a wedding – something that only the ‘rich people’ can afford.Yes, we know you can manage all those wedding arrangements yourself, but wouldn’t you want to keep your hands free and have a good time at a wedding ? If you really want to have fun at your wedding, without any worry or restrictions, then it would be a wise decision to rope in a good wedding planner!(This is applicable for budget weddings, too!)

A wedding planner would provide 360-degree services to organize your dream wedding for you.From taking care of the venue, along with the different vendors and services for your wedding, to accomodation, food, videography and / or photography, decor design and execution to traveling, and more, the planner will take care of every big and small detail on your behalf.

Wedding planners in India can cost anywhere between Rs 2 to 6 Lakhs.This cost can vary, depending upon the popularity of the planners, and the destination or the scale of the wedding.Please note – This is only the cost of the wedding planner’s services.Every other service that you avail for your wedding is a tie up with third party vendors that you will need to additionally pay for.
